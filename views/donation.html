Pawssion Project Foundation

Pawssion Project is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need. Founded in October 2018, our mission is to create animal-friendly communities that promote kindness to all living creatures. We strive to achieve "The Five Freedoms" for all stray animals through initiatives like daily stray feeding, regular spay and neuter services, and relief efforts during calamities. We believe in the power of compassion and work tirelessly to provide a second chance for abused, abandoned, and neglected animals.
